Emergency Stops (E-Stops) are critical safety devices designed to instantly remove hazardous energy. However, contacts can weld shut and mechanical buttons can seize. Ensure your facility has a documented schedule for testing E-Stops and safety interlocks under controlled conditions to verify they still break the circuit reliably.
