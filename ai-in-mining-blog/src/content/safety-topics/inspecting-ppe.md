Electrical PPE degrades over time and from physical damage. Every single day, before starting work, meticulously inspect your insulated tools, arc-rated clothing, and specially your rubber insulating gloves. Use the inflator tool or roll the glove to check for microscopic pinholes that could let current through. If in doubt, throw it out.
